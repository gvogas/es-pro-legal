ES Pro — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026 · Last updated: July 8, 2026

ES Pro is a scheduling tool that helps a business manage its appointments, clients, and employees. The App is operated by George Vogas. This policy explains what information the App handles, how it is used, and the choices you have. If you have questions, contact us at george@vogas.net.

1. Who this policy covers

The App is used by two kinds of people:

  • Account holders — the business owner/administrator and the employees they invite, who sign in to use the App.
  • Clients — the customers of that business, whose contact and appointment details are entered into the App by the account holders. Clients do not sign in to the App themselves.

For client data, the business that uses the App is the party that decides what is collected and why. We process that data on the business’s behalf to provide the service.

2. Information we collect

Account information

  • Email address and password (passwords are handled by Firebase Authentication and are never visible to us in plain text).
  • Display name, phone number, and role (administrator or employee).

Business and client records you enter

  • Client name, phone number, email, and address.
  • Appointment details: date/time, assigned employees, status, notes, and location.
  • Photos you attach to appointments or profiles.

Device permissions (used only when you choose the feature)

  • Camera — to take appointment or profile photos.
  • Photo library — to attach existing images.
  • Contacts — to open a pre-filled contact card so you can save a client to your phone’s contacts. The App does not read or upload your contact list.
  • Face ID / biometrics — to unlock the optional in-app lock you enable in Settings. Biometric data never leaves your device and is handled entirely by iOS; we never receive it.

Address lookup

When you type an address, the App sends the partial text to Google Maps Platform (Places API) through our secure backend to return suggestions. We do not send this through advertising services.

Diagnostics and crash data

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and basic diagnostic information (device model, operating system version, and the technical state at the time of a crash) so we can fix problems. This data is used solely to keep the App stable and is not used for advertising.

Accounting sync (administrators only)

If an administrator connects the optional Wave Accounting integration, client records (name, contact details) may be synced to the business’s Wave account to create or update customers there. This happens only when an administrator enables it.

3. How we use information

  • To provide core scheduling features — accounts, appointments, clients, and employees.
  • To authenticate users and control who can see which appointments.
  • To store and display the photos and records you create.
  • To verify that requests come from a genuine, untampered copy of the App (Apple App Attest / Firebase App Check).
  • To diagnose crashes and improve reliability.

We do not sell your data, we do not use it for advertising, and the App does not track you across other companies’ apps or websites.

4. How information is stored and protected

  • Data is stored in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore, Cloud Storage, Firebase Authentication) hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure.
  • Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest by the provider.
  • Access is restricted by security rules: an employee can see only the appointments assigned to them; administrative data requires an administrator account.
  • Backend requests require App Attest / App Check verification, which blocks requests from unverified or tampered clients.

5. Third parties that process data for us

We share data only with service providers that help us run the App:

  • Google (Firebase & Google Cloud) — authentication, database, file storage, crash reporting, and app-integrity checks.
  • Google Maps Platform — address autocomplete and place details.
  • Wave Financial (Wave Accounting) — optional customer sync, only if an administrator enables it.

These providers process data under their own privacy and security terms and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

6. Data retention

Account and business data is retained while the account is active. When you delete your account (see below), your account record is removed. Clients, appointments, and photos created within a business remain part of that business’s records unless deleted by the business. Crash diagnostics are retained by Firebase Crashlytics for a limited period per Google’s policies.

7. Your choices and rights

  • Delete your account. You can delete your account and associated profile from within the App at any time. This action is available in the App and does not require contacting us.
  • Access, correction, and deletion. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by emailing george@vogas.net. If you are a client of a business that uses the App, please also contact that business, as they control their records.
  • Permissions. You can revoke camera, photo, contacts, and Face ID permissions at any time in your device’s Settings; features that rely on them will be limited accordingly.

Depending on where you live (including Quebec and elsewhere in Canada), you may have additional rights under applicable privacy law. We will honor valid requests to the extent required by law.

8. Children

The App is a business tool intended for use by adults. It is not directed to children and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. International data transfers

Data may be processed and stored on servers located outside your province or country (for example, in the United States, where Google Cloud operates). Where required, we rely on our providers’ safeguards for such transfers.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, through the App or by other notice.

11. Contact us

Questions or privacy requests: george@vogas.net.
