Common questions

  • How do I get an account? ES Pro is invite-only. Your business administrator sends you an invitation with a one-time signup code; use that code, your email, and a password to create your account.
  • I forgot my password. On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot password to receive a reset email.
  • How do I delete my account? In the app, go to Settings → Account → Delete account. This removes your account and profile. Client and appointment records belong to the business and are managed by its administrator.
  • Notifications aren't arriving. Make sure notifications are enabled for ES Pro in your device Settings, and that you're signed in as an active employee.
